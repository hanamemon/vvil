Welcome to the Vital Voices Innovation Lab

As Vital Voices grows and evolves, its technology must grow and evolve with it. The Lab is a space for collaboration, contribution, and support designed to help the organization do exactly that. We are accessible to all Vital Voices network members and work collaboratively, ensuring everyone has the capability to either directly contribute or request support for challenging problems and ideas. Our Lab members are technical volunteers at the top of their fields, passionate about leveraging their skills to develop tech-enabled solutions that multiplies the reach and impact of the womxn leaders supported by Vital Voices, enabling them to achieve their goals quicker and more efficiently.
